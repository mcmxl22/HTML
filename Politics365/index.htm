My Body, My Choice

I don’t understand the argument about a woman’s right to choose what happens in her body. Why is the woman the only one who has that right? The right to choose what happens in ones own body makes a great argument for legalizing ALL drugs. If it’s my body, my choice, why am I being taxed for the sugar I eat? Isn’t that infringing on my right to choose what happens in my body? My body, my choice gets rid of most of the need for the FDA. If it’s my body, my choice then I never want to hear another criticism about what I eat!
